You Had Me at Hola follows Jasmine Lin Rodriguez, a Latina actress known for her tabloid-famous love life, and Ashton Suárez, a telenovela star from Puerto Rico guarding his privacy. Cast as leads in a new bilingual rom-com series, they agree to keep things professional. That lasts about five minutes. The novel is a celebration of Latinx culture, bilingual code-switching, and the romance genre itself.

The novel has been challenged for sexual content and profanity. A USA Today bestseller and a Today Show Read with Jenna pick, it has been removed from library shelves in Texas and Florida.
