The Fascinators follows Sam, a gay teenager in a small Southern town who practices magic with his two best friends as part of an after-school club. When a new boy arrives and Sam falls for him, the balance of their friendship shifts. Meanwhile, a national anti-magic movement is growing, and the Fascinators' powers attract attention from both admirers and dangerous extremists.

The novel has been challenged for its LGBTQ+ content and its depiction of witchcraft and magic. It combines a queer coming-of-age story with supernatural elements, making it a dual target for objections based on both content categories.
