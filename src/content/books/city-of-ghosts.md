City of Ghosts follows Cassidy Blake, who nearly drowned and came back changed. She can now pull back the Veil between the living and the dead, crossing into a gray, ghost-filled world. When her parents (hosts of a ghost-hunting TV show) take her to Edinburgh, Scotland, Cass encounters spirits far more dangerous than any she's met before.

A bestselling middle-grade novel by the author of the Shades of Magic series, it has been challenged for occult themes and depictions of violence. The fact that a middle-grade ghost story aimed at 8-to-12-year-olds has been banned speaks to the breadth of the current wave of book challenges.
