The Initial Insult is Edgar Allan Poe's "The Cask of Amontillado" reimagined as a YA thriller set in Amontillado, Ohio. Tress Montor's parents disappeared seven years ago, and she believes her former best friend Felicity Turnado knows what happened. On Halloween night, Tress lures Felicity into the basement of a condemned house and begins bricking up the wall, demanding the truth one brick at a time.

The novel has been challenged for graphic violence, drug references, profanity, and sexual content. Its dark, revenge-driven plot and Poe-inspired horror elements have made it a target for school library bans despite strong critical praise.
