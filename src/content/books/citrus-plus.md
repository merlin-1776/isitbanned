*Citrus Plus, Vol. 1* picks up after the main *Citrus* story and stays with the emotional fallout, the relationship strain, and the push-pull intensity that romance manga often thrives on. It is aimed at readers who want continuing character drama, not moral cleanliness.

## Why This Book Was Banned

The objections usually collapse queer romance and sexual content into the same bucket, even when the real issue is simply that the relationship is between girls. Manga also tends to get judged harshly by people who do not read it and assume style alone is evidence of danger.

## Why You Should Read This

One of the best reasons to read challenged manga is that it expands the map of what kinds of stories matter. Readers deserve access to romance, melodrama, and queer interiority too, not just books that pass an adult comfort test.
