The Inheritance Games follows Avery Grambs, a regular teenager who is stunned to learn that billionaire Tobias Hawthorne has left his entire fortune to her, a stranger. The catch: she must live in Hawthorne House for a year, surrounded by the disinherited Hawthorne grandsons who want answers. The mansion is filled with puzzles, riddles, and deadly secrets, and Avery must solve them all to survive.

A #1 New York Times bestseller, the novel has been challenged for violence and profanity. Its popularity among teen readers has kept it on bestseller lists for years, even as some school districts have moved to restrict access.
