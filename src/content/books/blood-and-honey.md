Blood & Honey continues the story of Lou and Reid from Serpent & Dove. Lou is a witch, Reid is a witch hunter, and their fragile alliance has shattered. As war brews between the witches and the Church, Lou must embrace the magic she has been running from, while Reid must decide how far he'll go to save the woman he loves. The stakes are higher, the magic darker, and the body count steeper.

The sequel has been challenged for explicit sexual content, graphic violence, and depictions of witchcraft. The Serpent & Dove trilogy has been a consistent target for school library bans, appearing on PEN America's index in multiple states.
