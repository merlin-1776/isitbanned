The Hand on the Wall is the thrilling conclusion to Maureen Johnson's Truly Devious trilogy. Stevie Bell, a true-crime-obsessed teenager at the elite Ellingham Academy in Vermont, finally closes in on the solution to both a present-day murder and the legendary 1936 Ellingham kidnapping. With a blizzard trapping everyone on campus, the stakes are life and death.

The novel has been challenged for violence, LGBTQ+ content (including a bisexual protagonist), and drug references. The entire Truly Devious series has appeared on PEN America's Index of School Book Bans.
