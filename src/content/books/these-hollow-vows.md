These Hollow Vows follows Brie, a mortal girl who has been stealing to support her sister. When her sister is abducted to the fae realm, Brie strikes a bargain with a faerie prince to get her back. Pulled into the glittering and dangerous Unseelie Court, she finds herself caught between two princes with competing agendas, and discovers that the fae world's politics are far deadlier than any human scheme.

The novel has been challenged for sexual content and violence. Part of the "romantasy" boom in YA publishing, it has been removed from school libraries in Texas and Florida.
