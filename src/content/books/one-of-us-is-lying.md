One of Us Is Lying is The Breakfast Club meets a murder mystery. Five students walk into detention, but only four walk out alive. Simon, the school's gossip app creator, dies, and the other four, Bronwyn the brain, Addy the beauty, Nate the criminal, and Cooper the athlete, all had reasons to want him dead. As secrets unravel, each student's hidden life is exposed.

A #1 New York Times bestseller adapted into a Peacock series, the novel has been frequently challenged for its sexual content, references to drug use, profanity, and depictions of violence. It appears on PEN America's Index of School Book Bans and has been removed from school libraries across multiple states.
