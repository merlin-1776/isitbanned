Eliza and Her Monsters follows Eliza Mirk, a shy, reclusive teenager who is secretly the anonymous creator of Monstrous Sea, one of the most popular webcomics on the internet. When new kid Wallace transfers to her school and turns out to be her favorite fanfiction writer, Eliza's carefully separated worlds begin to collide. The story blends prose, webcomic panels, and online chat formats to explore anxiety, creativity, fandom, and the terror of being truly seen.

The novel has been challenged for sexual content, depictions of self-harm and suicidal ideation, and profanity. Its honest portrayal of social anxiety and the importance of online communities to isolated teens has made it both a beloved favorite and a target for removal from school shelves.
