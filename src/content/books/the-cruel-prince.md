The Cruel Prince follows Jude Duarte, a mortal girl who was stolen away to the High Court of Faerie as a child. Now seventeen, she wants nothing more than to belong in a world that despises humans. But Prince Cardan, the youngest and wickedest son of the High King, makes her life miserable. To win her place at court, Jude must defy him, and in doing so, she becomes entangled in palace intrigue, power plays, and a dangerous game she might not survive.

A New York Times #1 bestseller and one of the most popular YA fantasy series of the decade, The Cruel Prince has been challenged for its depictions of violence, sexual content, and strong language. It appears on PEN America's index of banned books and has been pulled from school libraries in multiple states.
