A Pho Love Story is a Romeo and Juliet-inspired YA romance set in the Vietnamese American community. Bao Nguyen and Linh Mai are the children of families who own rival pho restaurants and have feuded for decades. When they meet and fall for each other, they have to navigate family loyalty, cultural expectations, and the secret of their relationship.

The novel has been challenged for sexual content and profanity. A lighthearted romance celebrated for its Vietnamese American representation, it has been pulled from school library shelves in Texas and Florida.
