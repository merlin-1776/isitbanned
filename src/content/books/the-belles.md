The Belles is set in the world of Orléans, where everyone is born gray and ugly, and only the Belles, magical women who can manipulate beauty, can give people color and attractiveness. Camellia Beauregard is a Belle who dreams of being chosen as the favorite of the queen. But when she arrives at court, she discovers that the world of beauty is built on cruelty, corruption, and a horrifying secret.

The novel has been challenged for its sexual content and depictions of violence. Author Dhonielle Clayton, who also co-founded We Need Diverse Books, has been a vocal advocate against book bans. The Belles appears on PEN America's Index of School Book Bans.
