The Project follows Lo Denham, whose life fell apart after her parents died in a car accident. Her older sister Bea, once her rock, has been swallowed whole by a group called The Unity Project, led by the magnetic Lev Warren. When Lo lands a journalism internship, she begins investigating the group and its leader, uncovering a trail of manipulation, abuse, and violence that leads right to her sister.

The novel has been challenged for violence, sexual content, and profanity. Courtney Summers is known for unflinching YA thrillers that tackle difficult subjects, and The Project's portrayal of cult dynamics and predatory charisma has landed it on PEN America's banned book index.
