Skinny Dipping follows Auden, a teenage girl who has grown up at her family's nudist resort but keeps that part of her life hidden from her school friends. When a cute new employee starts working at the resort, Auden's two worlds threaten to collide. It's a body-positive romantic comedy that treats nudity as normal rather than scandalous.

The novel has been challenged for sexual content and profanity. Despite being a lighthearted romcom, the premise of a nudist resort setting has made it a target for book challenges in multiple school districts.
