This book is exactly what the title says it is: a straightforward guide to puberty, anatomy, body changes, and the awkward questions kids often have before adults are ready to answer them. Its purpose is practical. It gives readers language for what is happening to them.

## Why This Book Was Banned

Books like this get challenged under the label of "sexual content," but that phrase blurs an important distinction. Explaining puberty is not the same thing as eroticizing it. A lot of these bans are really about adults wanting control over when, how, and whether young people get accurate health information.

## Why You Should Read This

A useful book can lower panic. It can replace rumor with language and embarrassment with understanding. That may sound unglamorous, but it's one of the most basic services a book can provide.
