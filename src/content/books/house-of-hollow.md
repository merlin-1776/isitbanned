House of Hollow follows the three Hollow sisters, who vanished as children and were found a month later with no memory of where they'd been. Now teenagers, they're magnetic, beautiful, and strange. Flowers bloom where they walk. People become obsessed with them. When the eldest sister, Grey, disappears again, Iris must follow a trail of gore, glamour, and dark fairy tale logic to find her, uncovering the terrifying truth about what the sisters really are.

The novel has been challenged for its graphic violence, sexual content, and dark supernatural themes. A gothic horror-fantasy that was a Kirkus and SLJ Best Book of the Year, it has been pulled from school libraries in Texas and Florida.
