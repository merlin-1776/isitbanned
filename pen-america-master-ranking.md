# PEN America Master Ranking — Banned Books by Popularity

## Methodology

Books ranked using a weighted composite of:
1. **General notoriety/cultural recognition** (highest weight) — classics, bestsellers, award winners, cultural touchstones rank first
2. **Total ban/challenge incidents** (PEN America 2021-2025 cumulative + ALA data)
3. **Goodreads ratings count** (proxy for readership size — estimated from known data)
4. **Recency of ban activity** (2023-2025 bans weighted higher for SEO trending potential)

### Data Sources
- PEN America Top 52 Most Banned Books 2021-2025 (cumulative ban counts)
- PEN America Index of School Book Bans: 2021-22, 2022-23, 2023-24, 2024-25
- ALA Top 10 Most Challenged Books: 2022, 2023, 2024
- ALA Top 100 Most Challenged Books by Decade (2010-2019, 2000-2009)
- Wikipedia: List of most commonly challenged books in the United States
- College Transitions, Reader's Digest, NBC News banned books compilations

### Sensitive Content Flags
- 🔫 = School shooting / gun violence
- ⚠️ = Suicide / self-harm
- 🔞 = Sexual content / assault
- 👶 = Child abuse / exploitation
- 🏳️‍🌈 = LGBTQ+ themes (reason for banning)
- 🎭 = Racial themes / racism
- 💊 = Drugs / substance abuse
- 🔒 = Trafficking / captivity
